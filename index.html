Katilruk

Fiction grounded in reality

Welcome! Unless you're one of my close friends or acquaintances, I'm not sure how you got here... Well, since you're here, let me tell you about my project!

The Katilruk Project is an ambitious project of mine, which I have been intermittently working on since around 2017, slowly developing it from a child's simple fantasy, to a highly-detailed world, with history spanning millennia, unique cultures, and even a unique species in place of humans!

Unfortunately, I am not yet ready to reveal it to the world; it is like a son to me, and I only want to release it once I feel it is developed enough. I plan to slowly reveal the world through books, from short stories, to epic novels! I also intend to make digital copies completely free, as this project is purely one of passion. However, if certain demand is met, I will offer physical copies for purchase.

But fret not! If you're interested in the project, feel free to bookmark this page and return periodically, or subscribe to one of the social media accounts I have set up for the project... Which I may or may not use... I guess I'll have to now!
